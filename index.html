Compositionality and Reasoning in AI and Cognitive Science

One‑day workshop — January 8, 2026 — Warsaw

About the workshop

This one‑day workshop brings together researchers in cognitive science and AI to discuss compositionality, structured representation, and symbolic reasoning in modern learning systems. We welcome contributions from theoretical, computational, and experimental perspectives.

Who should attend

Researchers, PhD students, and practitioners interested in the intersection of compositionality, reasoning, and cognitive models of intelligence.

  • 12:30–13:30 — Lunch break
  • 13:30–15:30 — Poster / demo session
  • 15:30–17:00 — Panel discussion & closing
