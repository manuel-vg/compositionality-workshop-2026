Compositionality and Reasoning in AI and Cognitive Science

Warsaw, January 8, 2026

About the Workshop

This one‑day workshop aims to discuss compositionality, structured representation, and symbolic reasoning in modern learning systems.

Topics include:

  • Hybrid Models for Natural Language Reasoning
    Neural models have made remarkable strides, but their ability to generalize in logical reasoning remains limited. Compositionality and recursiveness are distinct challenges: large language models perform well in the latter but struggle with the former. This topic examines a hybrid neural-symbolic approach that merges efficiency with rigorous inference, offering a path toward more robust reasoning systems.
